Privacy Policy

At Botify, we are committed to protecting the privacy and security of our users. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose information about our users and their use of our service.

Collection of Information

Personal Information: We may collect personal information, such as your name and email address, when you sign up for our service or contact us for support.

Website Link, Documents, and Media Files: We collect website links, documents, and media files that you provide to us in order to create chatbots for you.

Usage Information: We may collect information about how you use our service, such as the types of chatbots you create and the frequency and duration of your use.

Use of Information

To provide and improve our service: We use the information to operate and maintain our service, to provide customer support, and to improve the user experience.

To communicate with you: We may use your email address to send you information about our service and to respond to your inquiries.

To create chatbots: We use the information you provide to create chatbots for you.

To comply with legal obligations: We may use the information to comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal processes.

Sharing of Information

With third-party service providers: We may share your information with third-party service providers who help us operate our service and provide customer support.

With affiliates: We may share your information with our affiliates for marketing and other purposes.

In connection with a business transaction: If we are involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or a portion of our assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

With your consent: We may share your information with your consent.

For legal purposes: We may share your information if we believe it is necessary to comply with applicable laws, regulations, or legal processes.

Data Security

We take appropriate measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on our website.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us at support@botverse.click.
